Established in 1983 , Swastik Filaments is a pioneer in Indian Fishnet Manufacturing and Nylon Monofilament Yarn manufacturing. Using over three decades of expertise and the latest technologies, we produce top quality products for the Indian and Asian markets.


Additionally, Swastik Filaments also manufactures High Density Poly Ethylene Nets (HDPE) of various colours and sizes. While we are primarily focused on the Fishing Industry we also make yarn and nets for other purposes like Safety Nets, Cricket and Tennis Nets etc...


With three brands under our umbrella - Star, Mercury and Paris we produce nets for a variety of specifications. We operate 5 units spread across Tamil Nadu and Puducherry and are well equipped to serve all your yarn and net needs


A founding member of the Indian Fishnet Manufacturers Association, we have a reputation of reliability and high quality products in the industry. Using a variety of German, Japanese and Taiwanese machines, we strive to produce durable and efficient products
